about eme welding

At Eme Welding Australia, we believe great workmanship is more than just strong welds—it’s about building trust, reliability, and long‑lasting solutions. Since 2015, our team has been providing expert welding, fabrication, engineering, and mechanical maintenance services to businesses and projects across Botany, Matraville, and greater Sydney.

Led by founder Nick Elliott, who brings nearly two decades of industry experience, we pride ourselves on delivering cost‑effective, high‑quality results tailored to each client’s needs. Whether you require custom metal fabrication, structural engineering support, or ongoing machinery maintenance, our skilled professionals ensure every project is completed with precision and care.

With a reputation for free estimations, transparent communication, and client satisfaction, Eme Welding has become a trusted partner for construction companies, industrial operators, and local businesses. Our commitment to combining modern techniques with traditional craftsmanship means you can rely on us for durable, efficient, and professional solutions—every time.
